Pub Trivia

Pub trivia has been stumping the minds of drinkers for years. Originally started in England and Ireland, pub trivia made its way to the US through Irish immigrants in Philadelphia. It has since been a exciting bar room entertainment option across the country. Try it any night of the week. Our hosts will tease guests with 6 rounds of trivia with a wide array of trivia so everyone can play. We even include photo, video and music rounds to keep things exciting.
